Evaluation Programs

Art Bridges believes in the power of evaluation to create meaningful change in the field. We provide support for evaluation activities that allow museums to better understand their audiences, measure program impact, and make responsive visitor-based decisions.

Getting Started with Evaluation

The Art Bridges Collection contains over 200 artworks that are available for loan to museums across the United States. We believe art is meant to be seen, but we also recognize that there are many barriers to borrowing works of art, one of the largest being financial. For this reason, we ensure that loans from our collection are free of cost to borrowing venues.

Services Supported by Art Bridges

  • Evaluation Awards

    Front-End, Formative, and Summative Evaluation support for your projects.

  • Consultation Services

    Expert guidance on Outcomes, Goals, and Measurements for your programs.

  • COVES Program

    Comprehensive audience research and evaluation framework.

Note: The above list is not exhaustive. We encourage you to reach out to our team with any ideas you have to evaluate your Art Bridges project(s).

Visitor Experience Studies

COVES Spotlight

Our commitment to access includes access to insightful, actionable data. COVES is designed to help your museum measure impact and ensure meaningful, accessible art experiences.

COVES (Collaboration for Ongoing Visitor Experience Studies) is a validated survey tool that allows museums to learn directly from visitors about their museum experience. Including ongoing information about who visitors are, what their motivations for visiting are, what they do during their visit, and how they feel about their experience.
