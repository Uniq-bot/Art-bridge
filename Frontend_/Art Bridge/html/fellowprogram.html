Art Pool Fellow Programs

Our Fellows Program is dedicated to developing inclusive
museum leadership with a national group of emerging arts
professionals and mentors.

The program began as a three-year pilot to establish career pathways for historically underrepresented groups
in the arts and to offer new models for fellowship roles in museums. We launched the third year and the
evaluation of the program in the fall of 2024, and therefore we are not placing fellows at new locations. Check
back here for updates on the program or subscribe to our newsletter.

Partners hire fellows to work at their museums for three-year appointments. Each position is connected to
substantial, multiyear Art Pool programs—including major initiatives like the Cohort Program and Partner
Loan Network—and depends on the capacity needs of our partner museums.


In addition to ongoing professional development, the Art Pool Fellows travel to Bentonville for annual
gatherings as well as a monthlong residency and orientation in their first year.
