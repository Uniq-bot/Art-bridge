OUR MISSION

Art Pool delivers access to meaningful experiences with art for personal and community wellbeing.

We partner with museums of many sizes across the United States and its territories to provide strategic and financial support to get American art out of storage and into communities nationwide through our innovative and collaborative art sharing and community engagement

HOW WE FUND MUSEUMS

At Art Pool, we do things a little differently. We support partners and projects, rather than grantees and grants.

1

What We Fund

We provide museum partners with the financial, strategic, and logistical support needed to travel exhibitions, loan or borrow artworks, and develop related community programming.

2

Who Can Apply

We fund nonprofit organizations with 501(c)(3) status, state universities, state and city entities that meet eligibility requirements with the Internal Revenue Service, and government agencies. Please contact us, if you are unsure of your eligibility.

3

Funding Process

Once a project has been approved, the museum partner is invited to apply for funding and collaborate with our program and awards teams on an application.
