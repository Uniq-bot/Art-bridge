Community Engagement

Art Pool provides support for community engagement programs that allow museums to reach new audiences, gather meaningful visitor data, diversify the field, and break down barriers to access.

ABOUT

Our Community Engagement pillar encompasses five programs that allow our museum partners to:

Build community

We focus on projects that build trust, foster a sense of belonging, and establish lasting connections among audiences.

Support Interdisciplinarity

We support projects that integrate multiple disciplines, including performing arts, science, and technology.

Strengthen Partnerships

We champion collaborations with local artists, educators, and organizations to create meaningful cultural experiences.

Enrich Storytelling

We encourage programs that present fresh perspectives, amplify underrepresented voices, and have the potential to transform the field.

Learning & Engagement

Learning & Engagement awards help our museum partners create transformative programs that connect with new and diverse communities, actively engage current audiences in new ways, and highlight interdisciplinary elements. Partners with current collection placements, Partner Loan Network groupings, or traveling exhibitions, as well as those who are part of the Art Pool Cohort Program, are eligible to apply for Learning & Engagement funding.

Evaluation

Art Pool supports already planned or collaboratively designed program evaluation for partners that want to engage in front-end, formative, or summative evaluation; survey design; focus group facilitation; interview protocols and collection; data collection training; qualitative and quantitative data analysis; and behavioral observation and experimentation.

Fellows Program

Art Pool is dedicated to creating inclusive museum leadership with a national group of emerging arts professionals and mentors.
