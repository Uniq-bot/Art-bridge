Global Art Sharing

We are dedicated to fostering long-term art-sharing partnerships among museums of all sizes and locations throughout the United States and its territories.

HOW WE SHARE ART

We partner with museums to get artworks out of storage and into communities nationwide by providing financial and strategic support for collection loans, exhibitions, and art-sharing initiatives that broaden experiences with American art.

Lending Instutition

Lenders are typically museums with large or specialized collections. They are interested in sharing their collections with broader audiences and enthusiastic about collaborating with other museums across the country.

Installation of "American Revolutionaries: Art & Disruption" at Peoria Riverfront Museum, 2022

Browsing Venues

Borrowers are museums of all sizes across the country, including rural, urban, and suburban venues, that are dedicated to engaging their communities by providing greater access to American art.

Art Pool Collection

The Art Pool Collection represents an expanding vision of American art from the 19th century to present day and encompasses multiple media and voices. To date, the Art Pool Collection contains over 200 objects that are available for loan to museums across the United States. In addition to providing strategic support, Art Pool ensures that these collection loans are free of cost to borrowing venues.

Cohort Programs

The Art Pool Cohort Program expands access to American art by supporting multiyear, multi-institutional, art-sharing partnerships among museums nationwide. Each cohort, comprised of a lead museum and cohort members, works together for 4-6 years to share their collections, making art lending, borrowing, and display more efficient and environmentally sustainable. The goal of the program is to create self-sustaining ecosystems of art sharing, collaboration, and community engagement across the country.

Travelling Exhibitions

Through its traveling exhibitions program, Art Pool partners with institutions that have already organized or are in the process of developing great exhibitions but cannot tour them without additional financial, logistical, and strategic support. Qualifying borrowing venues are eligible for funding support to offset the cost of hosting the exhibition. Art Pool manages the tour and covers direct costs, including insurance, crating, packing, and shipping.
