Himalayan Art Festival at the Patan Museum

In collaboration with local artisans and cultural organizations, the Patan Museum's Himalayan Art Festival celebrates Nepal's rich artistic traditions, featuring live demonstrations of Thangka painting, metalwork, and wood carving, alongside contemporary interpretations of these ancient art forms.
