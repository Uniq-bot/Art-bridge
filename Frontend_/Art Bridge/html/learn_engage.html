Nepali Art Education &
Cultural Heritage

Preserving Nepal's Artistic Legacy

Discover the rich traditions of Nepali art through our educational programs. From Thangka painting workshops to traditional wood carving demonstrations, we connect communities with Nepal's vibrant artistic heritage. Our initiatives support both the preservation of ancient techniques and the evolution of contemporary Nepali art forms.

WHO CAN APPLY?

Museum partners with current or upcoming Art Bridges
Collection placements, Partner Loan Network groupings, or
traveling exhibitions, as well as those who are a part of the Art
Bridges Cohort Program, are eligible to apply for Learning &
Engagement support.

Work With Us

Community-building Support

We support projects that engage new and diverse audiences in sustained and
meaningful ways by funding direct program expenses, such as supplies,
honoraria, transportation, translation, and more!

Transformative Support

We support projects that transform the ways museums engage with their
communities through creative risk-taking and have the potential to
transform the museum field.

Interdisciplinary support

We support projects that integrate multiple disciplines—including the
performing arts, science, technology, and others—to expand access and
audience engagement within the visual arts.

Our programs are designed to make Nepali art accessible to all. Whether you're a student, artist, or art enthusiast, we offer resources to explore and appreciate Nepal's artistic traditions. Our approach combines traditional learning with innovative methods to keep these art forms alive for future generations.


For more information about our Nepali art education programs, or to inquire about workshops and resources, please contact our Education Department at education@nepalartheritage.org.

FAQs

How many programs can Art Bridges support per art-sharing project?

We can support one program, or pieces of many programs, whether
they happen onsite at your museum or offsite as part of your
community engagement strategy. Each museum determines its own
needs.

Does Art Bridges fund the entirety of a project?

Art Bridges does not fund 100% of program costs. We expect each
museum to contribute proportionally to the entire cost of the project
based on its operating budget.

What isn't covered by Learning & Engagement funding?

We do not fund staff salaries, catalogues, materials that will be sold, or
costs associated with fundraisers, exclusive member events, or
existing marketing initiatives in your operational plan/budget.
