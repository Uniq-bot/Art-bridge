Art Bridges Learning &
Engagement

The Learning & Enagagement team helps our
museum partners create transformative programs
that connect with diverse communities, actively
engage audiences in new ways,and highlight
interdisciplinary connections.

WHO CAN APPLY?

Museum partners with current or upcoming Art Bridges
Collection placements, Partner Loan Network groupings, or
traveling exhibitions, as well as those who are a part of the Art
Bridges Cohort Program, are eligible to apply for Learning &
Engagement support.

Work With Us

Community-building Support

We support projects that engage new and diverse audiences in sustained and
meaningful ways by funding direct program expenses, such as supplies,
honoraria, transportation, translation, and more!

Transformative Support

We support projects that transform the ways museums engage with their
communities through creative risk-taking and have the potential to
transform the museum field.

Interdisciplinary support

We support projects that integrate multiple disciplines—including the
performing arts, science, technology, and others—to expand access and
audience engagement within the visual arts.

Overall, we want to help you take creative risks and build your audience. We also want this process to be as
stress-free and enjoyable as possible. Think of us as both your advocate and the support you need to try new
things. We want to champion and move your great ideas forward.


For more information, or if you have any questions, please contact Jasmine DeJackome, Learning &
Engagement Manager, at education@artbridgesfoundation.org.
