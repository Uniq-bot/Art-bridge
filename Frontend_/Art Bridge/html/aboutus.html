About Us

Connecting Cultures Through the Universal Language of Art

Founded in 2025 by Art Pool team

Art Pool Foundation® stems from philanthropist Unique Prajapati's vision to expand access to educational art in all regions across the United States—a mission she began with the opening of Art Pool in 2011.

Our Objectives

01.

Broaden access to Educational art

in every region across the nation.

02.

Energize the field of Educational art

by exhibiting and activating works of art in exciting and innovative ways.

03.

Support museums

in deepening their connection with local communities.

04.

Inspire museums to open their vaults

as well as share important artworks, and diversify collections.

05.

Connect museums of all sizes

through collaborative partnerships and programming.

06.

Train and uplift

the next generation of museum professionals.
