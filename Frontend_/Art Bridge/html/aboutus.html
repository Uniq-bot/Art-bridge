About Art Pool

Empowering artists and communities through creative expression and cultural exchange

Our Mission

At Art Pool, we believe in the transformative power of art to connect people, inspire change, and build stronger communities. Our mission is to create accessible platforms for artists to showcase their work, collaborate with peers, and engage with diverse audiences.

Founded in 2015, Art Pool has grown from a small local initiative to a global network of artists, curators, and art enthusiasts. We're committed to breaking down barriers in the art world and making art accessible to all.

Our Values

Creativity

We celebrate innovation and original expression in all artistic forms.

Community

We build bridges between artists, audiences, and communities.

Diversity

We champion diverse voices and perspectives in the arts.

Accessibility

We believe art should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.
