Art Bridges Collection

The Art Bridges Collection represents an expanding vision of American art from the 19th century to present day and encompasses multiple media and voices.

About

The Art Bridges Collection contains over 200 artworks that are available for loan to museums across the United States. We believe art is meant to be seen, but we also recognize that there are many barriers to borrowing works of art, one of the largest being financial. For this reason, we ensure that loans from our collection are free of cost to borrowing venues.

How It Works

We work closely with our partner museums to share art and increase accessibility. A partnership with us typically begins by having a conversation with our Curatorial team. At this time, a partner museum can request specific works, or we can offer suggestions for thematically relevant art. Once availability is confirmed, a partner museum submits an application. Program Support

Collection loans are available at no cost to borrowing venues.

We pay for direct costs relating to:


  • Two-way shipping of artwork(s) and related logistics
  • Insurance coverage (works remain under our policy)
  • Crating
  • Conservation
  • Courier expenses, if required
  • Art handlers, if required
  • Additional security, if required
